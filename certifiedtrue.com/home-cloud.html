Technology has forever changed how we think of pictures.

Gone are the days of film negatives that could prove a photo was authentic.

But the law and legal practice has not kept up...

and today’s world of digital evidence has become a minefield.

AI Deep Fakes

AI assisted software can edit images and even create entirely new photos and videos from scratch. Fake news, fake backgrounds, even fake people are now easy to manufacture and the internet is full of them.

Watch Video

Fraud in Insurance and even Science

At least 1 in 75 claims for property and casualty insurance submit fraudulent photos. Even in science publishing, a study of 20k published papers found 4% had "manipulated images that affected their conclusions".

Learn More

Metadata is stripped or edited

Even if the photo itself is authentic, do you really know when or where it was taken? Date, time and geo location can easily be spoofed or edited and many phones and emails automatically strip critical EXIF and metadata from images.
