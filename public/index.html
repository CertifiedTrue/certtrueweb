How does it work?

  • Capture
  • Store & Record
  • Manage
  • Authenticate

Sign into our CertifiedPhoto mobile app on your phone. Take a photo from within the app. In a secured folder in the phone, a cryptographic fingerprint with a unique CertifiedTrue ID is taken of the raw photo, metadata like geolocation and timestamp, as well as EXIF data like aperature speed and the phone model. The photo and its metadata are then uploaded into the cloud.

Learn More

The photo itself is archived into CertifiedTrue's image vault, where it is accessable via our mobile and web apps. The unique fingerprint ID of the photo and its metadata are recorded on our blockchain ledger, creating an immutable record.

Learn More

Your admin panel gives you the ability to manage the photos taken as well as users, clients and cases associated with them. Search or filter for a particular photo or a subject tag like #waterdamage. Make sure all relevant photos are in the appropriate case file and client folder. You can even see how many photos have been Certified or how many reports have been generated each month for each client to make billing easier.

Learn More

When you need an exhibit for a case, simply print out a CertifiedPhoto Report, with the image, metadata and CertifiedTrue ID and attach to your filing. To verify its authenticity, courts or opposing counsel need only to enter the unique CertifiedTrue ID on our verification page (like a postal tracking number) and the image and details will come back as CertifiedTrue.
